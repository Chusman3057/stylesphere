Return Policy

Hassle-Free Returns

At StyleSphere, we want you to shop with confidence. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, our hassle-free return policy ensures that you can return or exchange items easily.

Eligibility for Returns

  • Items must be returned within 30 days of the delivery date.
  • Products must be in their original condition, unworn, unwashed, and with all tags attached.
  • Items purchased during sales or with discounts are eligible for exchange only, unless otherwise stated.
  • Final-sale items (e.g., undergarments, swimwear, and clearance items) are non-returnable for hygiene reasons.

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Contact our customer service team at support@stylesphere.com or call us at 1-800-123-4567 to request a return.
  2. Provide your order number and a brief description of the reason for your return.
  3. Our team will guide you through the process and provide a return shipping label if applicable.

Refund Process

  • Once we receive your returned item(s), our team will inspect them to ensure they meet the eligibility criteria.
  • Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days after inspection.
  • Refunds will be credited to the original payment method. Please note that shipping fees are non-refundable.

Exchange Policy

If you'd like to exchange an item for a different size, color, or style, please follow the same steps as outlined in the return process. Exchanges are subject to stock availability.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact our customer service team within 7 days of delivery. We’ll arrange for a replacement or refund at no additional cost to you.

Return Shipping

For returns due to size or preference, the cost of return shipping is the responsibility of the customer. If the return is due to an error on our part (e.g., wrong item shipped), we’ll cover the return shipping costs.

International Returns

For international orders, customers are responsible for return shipping costs and any customs or duties incurred. Refunds will be processed once the items are received and inspected.

Need Assistance?

If you have any questions or concerns about our return policy, feel free to reach out to our support team. We’re here to help make your shopping experience at StyleSphere seamless and enjoyable.
